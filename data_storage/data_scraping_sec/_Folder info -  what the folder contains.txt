What this folder (data_scraping_sec) contains: 
Data-tables scraped from sec.gov/EDGAR based on stock_list, these are the different forms that this folder should contain:
- 10K forms (Annual report)
- 8K forms (Current report)
- 10Q forms (Quaterly report)
- 4 (Insider trading Reports)
- 3 (Initial Insider holdings reports) (withdrawals)
